Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before starting your free trial.

Is there actually a free trial? Do I need a card?

Yes — 7 days completely free. Your card is stored securely via Stripe but not charged until the trial ends. Cancel any time with one click. No questions asked.

How is Synaptiq different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a general assistant. Synaptiq is trained specifically on A-Level Maths curricula — it knows AQA, Edexcel, OCR and WJEC mark schemes, shows working exactly how examiners expect it, and tracks your progress over time. It won't hallucinate a GCSE answer when you ask an A-Level question.

Which exam boards does Synaptiq cover?

AQA, Edexcel, OCR, and WJEC — all fully supported. You set your exam board during signup and every answer is aligned to that board's mark scheme style.

Can I use Synaptiq for both Year 12 and Year 13?

Yes. The full content library covers Pure 1 & 2, Statistics Y1 & Y2, and Mechanics Y1 & Y2 — so whether you're starting AS or finishing A2, every topic is covered.

What if I get the same question wrong repeatedly?

Synaptiq tracks your weak spots and surfaces them through the spaced-repetition flashcard system. The more you practice, the smarter your personalised revision plan becomes.

Is Synaptiq suitable if I have ADHD, dyslexia, or dyscalculia?

Yes — these are first-class features, not afterthoughts. ADHD mode breaks responses into shorter, focused steps. Dyslexia mode uses Lexend font with increased spacing. Dyscalculia mode adds colour-coded working and visual number lines.

How much does it cost after the trial?

£35/month, or £199/year (saving £221 — about £16.58/month). For context, a single hour with a private A-Level Maths tutor typically costs £40–80. Synaptiq gives you unlimited 24/7 access for less than the cost of a single tutoring session per month.

Can my school or college get Synaptiq?

Yes. We offer custom pricing for schools, sixth forms, and tuition centres with whole-class accounts, teacher dashboards, and invoice billing. Email schools@synaptiqai.co.uk or click "Book a Demo" on the pricing section.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Synaptiq — A-Level Maths AI Tutor

1. Data We Collect

We collect your name, email, learning profile (including year group, exam board, and any learning differences you disclose), and learning activity data including questions answered, AI tutor conversations, and uploaded resources (mark schemes and past papers).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to personalise your A-Level Maths learning experience. Learning difference information is used to adapt the AI tutor, font settings, question formatting, and accessibility features. Uploaded resources are used solely to improve the accuracy of AI tutor responses.

3. AI Conversations

Your conversations with the Synaptiq AI tutor are processed via the Anthropic API. These conversations are used to provide responses and are not stored permanently beyond your active session.

4. Uploaded Resources

Mark schemes and past papers you upload are stored securely and used only to provide context to the AI tutor for your sessions. They are not shared with other users or third parties.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to: access your data, correct inaccurate data, delete your data, export your data, and withdraw consent at any time. Contact us at privacy@synaptiqai.co.uk.

6. Payments

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store card details. Subscription billing is managed securely by Stripe in accordance with PCI DSS standards.

7. Data for Minors

For users under 13, we require parental consent. We collect minimal data and do not use it for any purpose beyond providing the educational service.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and optional analytics cookies (which you can decline). We do not use advertising cookies.
