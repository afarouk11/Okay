🔴 Security 8 issues ▶
Do before any growth
  • Auth & profile APIs — critical authentication/authorization gaps allowing potential privilege escalation (see internal security tracker)Critical
  • Email delivery — unauthenticated or weakly controlled email sending path that could be abused for spoofed messages (details tracked privately)Critical
  • AI/chat endpoints — missing or inadequate access controls leading to potential abuse of third‑party AI creditsCritical
  • Billing & payments — misconfigured access control/CORS around billing flows that could expose sensitive account actionsCritical
  • Voice/TT S integration — unauthenticated or weakly protected integration enabling potential quota or credit drainHigh
  • Token verification — legacy/demo tokens still accepted in production-like flows; harden verification logicHigh
  • Login flows — gaps around email verification and account confirmation requirementsHigh
  • Admin tooling — overly powerful administrative actions protected by weak secrets and lacking defense-in-depthHigh
  • /api/supabase-auth — upsert_profile, patch_profile, get_profile have zero auth. Any caller can escalate to is_admin=trueCritical
  • /api/resend — no auth, no rate limit. Anyone can send Synaptiq-branded emails to any addressCritical
  • /api/chat — no auth gate. Caller controls model + max_tokens. Unauthenticated Anthropic credit burnCritical
  • /api/stripe — CORS wildcard hardcoded. Billing portal accessible by any caller who knows an emailCritical
  • /api/tts — no auth. Unauthenticated ElevenLabs quota drainHigh
  • /api/auth verify — accepts demo_token_ strings even when Supabase is fully configuredHigh
  • /api/auth login — auto-confirms unverified emails, bypassing email verification entirelyHigh
  • /api/admin — reset_users deletes all accounts with only a static key and a confirm() dialog. No timing-safe key comparisonHigh
