How to use Voice Mode

1
Allow microphone access

Your browser will ask for mic permission. Click "Allow" — this lets J.A.R.V.I.S. hear you.

2
Say "Jarvis" to wake the AI

The orb will flash white and connect. You'll see SESSION ACTIVE in the status panel.

3
Ask your maths question naturally

Try: "Can you explain integration by parts?" or "Help me solve this differentiation problem."

4
End the session when done

Press the END SESSION button, or simply stop talking. J.A.R.V.I.S. will save your progress.

⚠️ Voice mode requires Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Firefox and Safari don't support the Web Speech API used for wake-word detection. Use Text Chat if you're on another browser.
